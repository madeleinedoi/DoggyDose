About Us!

Just four dog lovers sitting at a table at 7 am! There are not enough dog appreciation websites in this world. Sophia, Madeleine, Ella, and Elsie set out to change the world through DoggyDose. Our website will give you a daily dose of dogs...a smile, a laugh, and a giggle to make your day!!
