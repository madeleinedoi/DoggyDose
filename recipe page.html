Pumpkin Spice Doggy Pupcakes!

    Ingredients:

    For the cupcakes:
        ½ cup whole grain oat flour
        1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
        1 teaspoon baking powder
        1 egg
        1 tablespoon maple syrup
        1 tablespoon (28 g) peanut butter
        ½ cup (113 g) pumpkin pureé
        ½ cup (50 g) shredded carrot
        ¼ cup (57 g) greek yogurt

    For the Peanut Butter Frosting
        ½ cup (114 g) greek yogurt
        1/4 cup (2 oz or 57 g) peanut butter
        ½ tablespoon maple syrup

    Instructions:

    1. Preheat oven to 350° F.
    2. Spray a muffin tin with nonstick spray. Set aside.
    3. In a medium bowl, whisk all the ingredients together until well incorporated, about 2 minutes.
    4. Scoop batter evenly into prepared tin. Bake for 35-40 minutes until firm. Cool completely.
    5. Place all of the frosting ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
    6. Transfer mixture to a frosting bag with desired tip. Pipe frosting onto cooled cupcakes
    7. Enjoy!

Peanut Butter, Banana and Yogurt Popsicles 

    Ingredients:
    
        18 oz plain nonfat yogurt
        1/2 cup peanut butter
        4 oz jar of banana baby food
        1 tablespoon of honey


    Instructions:

    1. Begin by mixing the yogurt -- make it healthy for your pup.

    2. Then, add  peanut butter and stir until the peanut butter blends with the yogurt.

    3. Now, mix in banana baby food

    4. Finally, sweeten the popsicle blend with the honey and stir until everything is smooth and creamy.

    5. Fill a small plastic cup to the top with the popsicle mix and stick a dog bone half way into the mixture to serve as the handle

    6. Stick the popsicles in the freezer and wait until completely frozen to serve to your dog.

    7. Enjoy!!
